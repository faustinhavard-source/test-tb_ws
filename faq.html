FAQ

Is this an accelerator?
We help you find a co founder and launch your company. You can come with or without an idea. Most will come solo but if you're in a team lets chat.
How much equity do you take?
We invest up to $250k on simple terms: $125k for 8% via an EF SAFE, plus an optional $125k MFN SAFE.
Do I need an idea or a cofounder?
No. Most arrive solo and form teams here. Ideas evolve, so do teams.
I'm not in Europe. Can I still apply?
Yes. But if you're already in the US or Asia, check out the EF programmes already running in those regions instead.
Is this only for CS or "AI people"?
No. We back builders across software, robotics, and other weird tech. If you can ship, you're welcome. We also back people who can grow, sell and distribute.
What happens after 8 weeks?
You pitch for $250k in pre-seed funding, keep building in the US, and plug into our EF program and office in San Francisco. There's no limit on the number of companies we can invest in - we're just looking for great teams.
Do you help with visas / banking / phones / the boring stuff?
Yes. We'll land you softly so you can sprint from day one. We'll even cover your Pringles in the plane if you're exceptional.
Visas are scary. How real is the support?
Very. We start paperwork the moment you're in and walk you through it.
How competitive is this?
EF's existing programmes have an X% acceptance rate. We expect this to be more competitive - there are only 40 spots.
Where exactly is the house?
Bay Area. Address shared with accepted founders only.
What does this cost to attend?
We cover the residency: housing, workspace, food, program.
I'm not ready yet.
Meet us on tour, get feedback, and we'll let you know what we think.
